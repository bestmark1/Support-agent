# Initial FitMentor KB Schema

## Priority Rule

For refund-related answers, the priority order is:

1. FitMentor `Refund Policy`
2. FitMentor `Terms of Use`
3. FitMentor landing page and contacts pages
4. T-Bank acquiring documentation as operational reference only

T-Bank documentation may help explain payment and refund mechanics, but it does not override FitMentor's own refund policy.

## Purpose

This is the minimal approved knowledge base for the first FitMentor support workflow.

It is intentionally small and should be filled from real public sources already available:

- landing page
- privacy policy
- terms of use
- refund policy
- contacts page
- selected T-Bank acquiring documentation for payment/refund support context

## Initial Cards

### 1. What FitMentor is

- category: `product`
- title: `What is FitMentor`
- source:
  - landing page
- approved summary:
  - FitMentor is an AI nutrition coach inside Telegram.
  - It is chat-first and is designed for nutrition and habit tracking without installing a separate app.

### 2. How FitMentor works

- category: `product`
- title: `How FitMentor works in Telegram`
- source:
  - landing page
  - FAQ
- approved summary:
  - Users interact with FitMentor directly in Telegram.
  - They can log food, water, weight, activity, and sleep in chat.
  - No separate app installation is required.

### 3. Free plan basics

- category: `billing`
- title: `What is included in the free plan`
- source:
  - landing page pricing block
  - FAQ
- approved summary:
  - The free plan is available forever.
  - It includes text food logging, water, weight, activity, and sleep tracking.
  - It includes 3 AI messages per day and a daily summary with calories, macros, and alerts.

### 4. Pro plan basics

- category: `billing`
- title: `What is included in the Pro plan`
- source:
  - landing page pricing block
  - terms of use
- approved summary:
  - Pro includes everything in Free.
  - It adds meal photos, voice input, 30 AI messages per day, weekly AI patterns, Sunday insights, and 3 personal recipes per week.
  - Public pricing shown on the site is 399 RUB per month or 2,999 RUB per year.

### 5. Premium plan basics

- category: `billing`
- title: `What is included in the Premium plan`
- source:
  - landing page pricing block
  - terms of use
- approved summary:
  - Premium includes everything in Pro.
  - It adds unlimited AI messages, unlimited recipes, mood-nutrition analysis, slip-risk alerts, and priority support.
  - Public pricing shown on the site is 999 RUB per month or 7,999 RUB per year.

### 6. Voice input availability

### 13. When a refund may be available

- category: `billing`
- title: `When a refund may be possible`
- source:
  - FitMentor refund policy
- approved summary:
  - Refunds are not the default outcome.
  - As a general rule, once a digital subscription has been activated and used, it may no longer be refundable unless required by law or due to a technical failure on FitMentor's side.
  - A refund may be reviewed manually if payment succeeded but the subscription was not activated.
  - A duplicate charge may also be reviewed for refund.

### 14. When a refund is normally not available

- category: `billing`
- title: `When refunds are usually not granted`
- source:
  - FitMentor refund policy
- approved summary:
  - If the subscription was activated and paid features were used, a refund is normally not available.
  - The main policy position is that refunds are generally not provided after activation and use.

### 15. How to request a refund

- category: `billing`
- title: `How to request a refund`
- source:
  - FitMentor refund policy
  - contacts page
- approved summary:
  - Refund requests should go through Telegram support or support email.
  - The user should include their Telegram account, payment date, amount, and the reason for the request.

### 16. Refund review timing

- category: `billing`
- title: `How long refund review and processing can take`
- source:
  - FitMentor refund policy
  - T-Bank documentation as secondary context
- approved summary:
  - If a refund request is approved, it is processed through the original payment channel.
  - FitMentor's policy says this usually takes up to 10 business days unless the payment method requires a different process.
  - Bank-side settlement timing may still vary after processing.

### 17. What to do if payment succeeded but access did not activate

- category: `billing`
- title: `Payment succeeded but subscription did not activate`
- source:
  - FitMentor refund policy
  - support contacts page
  - T-Bank documentation as secondary context
- approved summary:
  - This is a support case that should be reviewed manually.
  - Ask the user for their Telegram account, payment date, amount, and any payment confirmation details.
  - Do not promise an automatic refund.
  - Route the case to support review.

### 18. What to ask for in payment-related support cases

- category: `support`
- title: `What information to request for payment or refund issues`
- source:
  - FitMentor refund policy
  - operational support needs
- approved summary:
  - Telegram account
  - payment date
  - amount
  - reason for the request or problem description
  - if available, payment confirmation or receipt details
